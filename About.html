About Us


The Struggle

This project presented numerous challenges. Many of our members were new to hackathons, so the initial stages were marked by a steep learning curve. We encountered difficulties in adapting to web development intricacies specific to this project, and striking the right balance between user-friendly interfaces and resource optimization proved to be a significant challenge. Additionally, sourcing accurate data on food costs posed another hurdle, given the limited availability of comprehensive grocery item cost data. Nevertheless, with more time and effort, this project has the potential to evolve into a valuable nation-wide resource for mitigating food insecurity.

The Purpose

The alarming rise in food costs has triggered a rapid increase in food insecurity, a challenge that knows no boundaries and can affect anyone. Recent statistics shed light on the widespread nature of this issue, highlighting its profound impact on individuals and families across the nation. In the United States alone, prior to the pandemic, approximately 10% of households experienced food insecurity at some point during the year. This underscores the stark reality that millions of our fellow citizens, often living right in our neighborhoods, grapple with uncertainty about their next meal. The importance of addressing this crisis cannot be overstated, for it reminds us that food insecurity is not a distant concern but a pressing challenge that can touch the lives of anyone, regardless of their background or circumstances. As we come together to confront this issue, we have the opportunity to make a difference and ensure that no one in our communities goes to bed hungry

The Vision

We embarked on this journey with the goal of addressing a significant and widespread issue, one that hits close to home, especially for our fellow college students who often grapple with food insecurity. Inspired by this, we conceived REACH, a community-driven website designed to provide assistance to those in need. Our platform offers a range of valuable tools, including a budget calculator aimed at helping users achieve the most cost-effective yet nutritious food budget, the ability to create and estimate the cost of recipes, and a forum for those seeking support when budgeting isn't a viable option. In recognition of the alarming statistics on food insecurity post pandemic, we've also integrated features that connect users with food drives in their communities and encourage them to organize their own initiatives. Together, we can make a meaningful impact in alleviating the challenges of food insecurity faced by students and individuals alike.
